## When to Use
- Triggered every Sunday or on manual demand to summarize the week's output.

## Instructions
1. Read all files in `Done/` from the last week.
2. Review `Business_Goals.md` to align progress with goals.
3. Generate a markdown briefing in `Briefings/`.

## Validation
- [ ] Accurate list of activities.
- [ ] Professional and concise.
